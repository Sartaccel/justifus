About Mr. Asir Justifus

Founder & CEO of SART Group of Companies

Introduction

Mr. Asir Justifus, born on July 22, 1971, in Waterfall Estate, Valparai, a tea plantation company, based out of the Anaimalai hills, Pollachi, Coimbatore region in Tamil Nadu, India. He grew up in Santhapuram, a small village near Nagercoil in Kanyakumari district, Tamilnadu where he was deeply connected to the local traditions. His formative years inspired his entrepreneurial spirit and vision.

He pursued his higher education at Sivanthi Aditanar College, Nagercoil under Madurai Kamaraj University, where he completed his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. His academic excellence continued at Thanthai Hans Roever College in Perambalur affiliated with the Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirapalli where he earned his Master’s degree in Computer Science.

With over 30 years of experience in technology integration and more than two decades in logistics operations and entrepreneurship, Mr. Justifus has successfully led the SART Group of Companies as its Founder & CEO. His leadership journey extends beyond SART, as he has co-founded a handful of startup ventures, leveraging his diverse expertise to impact a wide range of industries including Logistics, Supply Chain, Transportation, Recruitment, Human Capital Management, Real Estate, High Tech Manufacturing to name a few. 

His career as a parallel entrepreneur has enriched his expertise, allowing him to consult with few leading companies including Union Motors Services (UNIPRO), Gillette, Deloitte, Amgen, NetApp, CISCO, Norfolk Southern, UPS, DHL, AT&T, Macys etc.

With a passion for innovation and a dedication towards customer acquisition and business growth, Mr. Justifus continues to be a visionary. He envisions transforming ideas into impactful solutions that shape the future of the startup companies he is associated with.

Experience

Mr. Asir Justifus is a dynamic  leader, consultant, and entrepreneur with expertise spanning IT, IoT, and Customer Services. During his early stage of his career, he has advised government, training and corporate sectors, enhancing operations and client relations. With this well rounded expertise and collective wisdom, the Ventures he has founded continues to reflect the same.

Mr. Asir Justifus began his career as a lab instructor & Faculty at Aptech in 1993, where he developed his foundational skills around Information Technolgy and the Digital Transformation wave. He keenly observed the  Globalization from early ‘90s.

With his involvement with  Nambawan (Formely Public Officers Superannuation Fund Board of Papua New Guinea) through Equinox Solutions, he deepened his technical expertise and analytical abilities. 

His journey then led him to Amgen and Netapp through Infogain where he consulted in TIBCO Enterprise Application Integration technology and Business Service Management for managing multi-billion dollar critical business service operations.

As technology advanced, Mr. Justifus expanded his skill set by specializing in the Internet of Things (IoT) at CISCO and AT&T, embracing the innovative technologies by connecting devices and systems towards Sales Enablement Tools.

His role at Allconnect involved migration of business-critical call center based on premises-based J2EE solutions to Amazon Cloud.

Through each position, Mr. Justifus has consistently adapted to evolving technologies and strengthened his expertise across IT, IoT, and cloud management. His career reflects a strong commitment to growth and innovation in the tech industry.

His consulting for Norfolk Southern strengthened their logistics and transportation systems. His career reflects a strong ability to support organizations with innovative IT strategies and practical solutions.

Leadership or Entrepreneurship experience

Mr. Asir Justifus, a visionary entrepreneur, has founded, co-founded, and directed several impactful ventures in the logistics, technology, and recruitment sectors. His impressive portfolio showcases his dedication to innovation and growth across various industries. 

Starting with Translogix, where he served as the Founder, President, and Chairman before it evolved into TruckerCloud, Mr. Justifus has continually led transformative initiatives. He also founded ELDMarketplace.com, reflecting his commitment to enhancing efficiency in logistics technology. His ongoing contributions include establishing TruckerGIG, a platform connecting top to bottom process in logistics and supply chain sectors, and Enterkey Solutions, a company providing comprehensive IT services.

In addition to these ventures, Mr. Justifus plays a pivotal role as the Founder and Advisor of TalentTurbo, a platform designed to empower job seekers while creating monetization opportunities. At SART Accelerator, where he is the Managing General Partner, he accelerates marketing and sales initiatives to boost startups’ success.

Mr. Justifus further extended his influence in logistics by founding Trusted Roadways and Logistics Private Limited and serving as a Director at United Technology Solutions (India) Private Limited. His expertise is also recognized internationally as a Board Member of the Logistics & Transportation Association of North America (LTNA), where he contributes to shaping the future of transportation and logistics.

Each role in Mr. Justifus' career underscores his unwavering commitment to building, advising, and advancing companies that drive progress and innovation across industries.
