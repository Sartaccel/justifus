Leadership & Entrepreneurship Experience

Mr. Asir Justifus, an esteemed leader and Entrepreneur in IT, IoT, and customer services, has guided his ventures with a dedication to innovation and strategic growth. His journey began as a lab faculty member, where he cultivated expertise in IT and digital transformation, eventually advancing to consult for major firms such as Amgen and NetApp. Over the years, he has contributed significantly to IoT advancements at CISCO and AT&T, as well as spearheading cloud migration projects at Allconnect. As the visionary founder behind ventures like TruckerCloud, ELDMarketplace.com, TalentTurbo, and TruckerGIG, Mr. Justifus is at the forefront of delivering transformative solutions in logistics and recruitment. His impact reaches an international scale, as he serves on the board of LTNA, driving forward innovation in logistics and transportation. Through these endeavors, Mr. Justifus continues to inspire and shape the future of multiple industries, leaving a legacy of progress and impact.
